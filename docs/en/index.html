How to delete your account

Attention: Irreversible Action

Account deletion is permanent and all your data will be permanently deleted, including medication history, schedules, and dependent accounts linked to your profile.

Step-by-step guide to delete your account

1

Open the MY MEDICINE app

Make sure you are logged in with the account you want to delete.

2

Access your profile settings

Tap on your profile picture, usually located in the upper corner of the main screen.

3

Scroll to the bottom of the page

Scroll down the settings screen until you find the account management section.

4

Click the "DELETE ACCOUNT" button

This button is located at the bottom of the settings page.

5

Confirm the deletion

Carefully read the warning about permanent data deletion and confirm your decision by clicking "DELETE MY ACCOUNT".

What will be lost with account deletion:

  • All medication history records
  • Scheduled reminders and alerts
  • Data of dependent accounts linked to your account
  • Customized application settings
  • Any other information associated with your account

Are you sure?
